JOB POST: safety patrol/Community Safety Volunteer Patroller

Places: 10 Job Type: Paid Volunteer Position

We are seeking dedicated individuals to serve as Community Safety Volunteer Patrollers in different communities across Gauteng. As a paid volunteer, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and security of our community through regular patrols and vigilance.

Job title: Community Safety Volunteer Patroller

Job Description:

Within reasonable range your duties will include but not limited to:

  • Conduct routine patrols in designated areas of the community to deter and report any suspicious or unsafe activities.
  • Act as a visible and approachable presence, assisting residents and authorities with safety concerns when necessary.
  • Document and report any incidents, hazards, or security breaches observed during patrols.
  • Collaborate with local authorities and community leaders to promote a safe and secure environment.

Requirements:

  • Strong commitment to community safety and the well-being of residents.
  • Excellent observational skills and attention to detail.
  • Good communication abilities to interact effectively with community members and authorities.
  • Availability to work shifts.
  • Must be between 18 and 40 year of age, and have a valid ID or Passport.

Salary and Benefits:

  • Compensation: R3200 per month (part-time, paid volunteer position).
  • Opportunity to make a meaningful contribution to the safety and security of our community.

How to Apply:

To apply for this position, please send your resume and a brief cover letter outlining your interest and relevant experience to:

[hr@haibo-community.co.za]. Join us in making a positive difference in our community's safety and security. Become a valued part of our team as a Community Safety Volunteer Patroller!
