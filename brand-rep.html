JOB POST: Brand Representative

Places: 2

We are looking for a dynamic and experienced Sales Manager to lead and manage a team of direct sales representatives engaged in door-to-door sales. The ideal candidate will be responsible for guiding, supporting, and supervising the sales team to ensure the achievement of sales goals and objectives.

Job title:Sales Manager (Field Sales)

Job Description:

Within reasonable range your duties will inclube but not limited to:

  • Team Supervision and Support:
  • Strategy Development
  • Territory Management
  • Sales Monitoring and Reporting
  • Training and Development
  • Relationship Building

Requirements:

  • Proven experience in sales management, preferably in a direct sales environment.
  • Strong leadership and motivational skills to drive a sales team.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities

  • Bachelor's degree in business, marketing, or a related field (preferred)
